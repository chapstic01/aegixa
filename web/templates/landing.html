Automod & Anti-Raid
11 configurable filters — spam, links, caps, invites, mentions, phishing detection (Premium) and more. Smart anti-raid with auto-kick/ban thresholds.
Advanced Logging
9 log categories: edits, deletes, member joins/leaves, voice events, role changes, channel updates, mod actions, and more — all routed to separate channels.
Ticket System
Button-based tickets with up to 3 configurable types, add/remove users, rename, staff notes, HTML transcripts, and auto-close idle tickets.
Moderation Suite
Kick, ban, mute, warn, temp-ban, message purge, channel lock, slowmode. Full warning history per member with reason tracking.
Join / Leave & Autoroles
Custom welcome and leave messages with rich variable support. Welcome DMs. Auto-assign roles on join with configurable delay.

Create and manage giveaways with automatic winner selection, reroll, and end announcements. Fully button-driven.
